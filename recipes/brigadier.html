Brigadier

Description

Is a brazilian chocolate candy.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of condensed milk.
  • 7 tablespoons of chocolate milk or 4 tablespoons of chocolate powder.
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted margarine.
  • chocolate sprinkles to taste.

Steps

  1. In a deep pan, add the condensed milk, margarine and chocolate powder.
  2. Cook over medium heat and stir until the brigadeiro starts to come away from the pan.
  3. Let it cool and make small balls with your hands by passing the dough into the chocolate sprinkles.

Cheese Bread

Description

Is a typical food from the state of Minas Gerais in Brazil, is made of cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 american glasses of milk.
  • 2 american glasses of cooking oil.
  • 500 g of sweet cassava starch.
  • 3 integer eggs.
  • 1 american cup of water.
  • 1 soup spoon of salt.
  • grated cheese to taste

Steps

  1. boil milk with water and cooking oil.
  2. In a bowl mix the cassava starch and salt.
  3. Pour in the boiled liquid and mix with a large spoon.
  4. Wait for it to cool and pour in the grated cheese and eggs.
  5. Mix the dough by hand, kneading well until it becomes a hard homemade glue.
  6. Make balls of whatever size you prefer.
  7. Bake in a very hot oven until golden.
  8. Serve warm.
